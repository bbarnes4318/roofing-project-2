## üí° PRO TIPS FOR KLINGAI

1. **Be Specific**: The more detail you provide, the better the output
2. **Use Visual Language**: Describe what you want to see, not just what you want to say
3. **Emphasize Transitions**: Smooth transitions make professional videos
4. **Include Text Overlays**: They help convey key messages
5. **Specify Camera Movements**: Dolly, crane, aerial shots add production value
6. **Color Grading**: Specify the mood through color descriptions
7. **Pacing**: Mix fast cuts with slow, cinematic moments
8. **Show, Don't Tell**: Visual demonstrations are more powerful than text
