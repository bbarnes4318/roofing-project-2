## üé§ Presentation Tips

### Voice & Delivery:
- **Speak clearly** - Enunciate, don't rush
- **Vary your tone** - Show enthusiasm for exciting features
- **Pause for effect** - Let important points sink in
- **Use "you" language** - "You can...", "This helps you..."
- **Tell stories** - "Imagine you're managing the Johnson project..."

### Screen Recording:
- **Move slowly** - Give viewers time to see what you're showing
- **Highlight with cursor** - Circle or point to important elements
- **Zoom in** - Use Loom's zoom feature for small text
- **Pause between sections** - Brief pause before moving to next topic
- **Show, don't just tell** - Click through features, demonstrate actions

### Energy Management:
- **Start strong** - First 30 seconds set the tone
- **Maintain energy** - Don't let your voice drop
- **Smile while talking** - It comes through in your voice
- **Take breaks if needed** - Pause recording, stretch, resume
- **End strong** - Closing is as important as opening
