**Kenstruction** is a revolutionary AI-powered project management platform specifically designed for roofing contractors. It represents a complete transformation from traditional manual project management to an intelligent, automated business operating system.

**Core Innovation**: The platform combines industry-specific workflow automation with an AI assistant (Bubbles) that can understand natural language, take actions, and communicate via text or voice - making it the first and only platform of its kind in the roofing industry.

**Purpose**: Automated workflow progression ensuring no step is ever missed

**How It Works**:

1. **Phases** (Top Level)
   - LEAD: Initial customer contact and information gathering
   - PROSPECT: Site inspection, estimates, insurance processing
   - APPROVED: Material ordering, permitting, scheduling
   - EXECUTION: Installation, quality checks, progress tracking
   - COMPLETION: Final inspection, payment, warranty

   - Groups of related tasks within each phase

   - Example: "Answer Phone Call ‚Äì Office üë©üèº‚Äçüíª"
